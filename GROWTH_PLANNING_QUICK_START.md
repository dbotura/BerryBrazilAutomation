# Growth Planning - Quick Start

## 📅 Planning Timeline

```
TODAY (March 2026)
    ↓
NEXT MONTH (April 2026) ← Start planning here
    ↓
Month 2 (May 2026)
    ↓
Month 3 (June 2026)
    ↓
...
    ↓
Month 24 (March 2028) ← Plan up to here
```

**You can plan customer growth from next month through 24 months ahead.**

## 🚀 Quick Setup (5 minutes)

### 1. Set Product Averages

Go to Growth Projections → Product Metrics tab

```
Açai Bowl 300g:     3 units/customer/month
Açai Smoothie:      2 units/customer/month
Açai Powder:        0.5 units/customer/month
```

### 2. Enter Customer Plan

Go to Customer Projections tab

```
Next Month (Apr):   2 new customers
Month 2 (May):      3 new customers
Month 3 (Jun):      2 new customers
Month 4 (Jul):      4 new customers
...continue through Month 24
```

### 3. View Forecast

Go to Sales Forecast tab or Forecasting page

See projected sales based on your growth plan!

## 💡 Example Scenario

**Your Plan:**
- Next month: Onboard 2 new customers
- Each customer buys 3 units/month on average

**What Happens:**

```
Next Month:
  Baseline: 450 units (from trend)
  New customers: 2 × 3 = 6 units
  Total: 456 units

Month 2:
  Baseline: 460 units
  Previous customers: 2 × 3 = 6 units
  New customers: 3 × 3 = 9 units
  Total: 475 units

Month 3:
  Baseline: 470 units
  All customers: (2+3+2) × 3 = 21 units
  Total: 491 units
```

**Cumulative Effect**: New customers keep buying every month!

## 🎯 Key Points

✅ **Start from next month** - Earliest you can add customers
✅ **Plan up to 24 months** - Long-term growth strategy
✅ **Update anytime** - Plans change, so can your projections
✅ **Cumulative impact** - New customers continue buying
✅ **Automatic conversion** - Works in weekly and monthly views

## 📊 Where to Use

1. **Growth Projections Page** - Enter and manage your plan
2. **Forecasting Page** - See impact on sales forecast
3. **Reports** - Track actual vs projected

## 🔄 Updating Your Plan

As your business evolves:

1. Go to Growth Projections
2. Click Edit on any month
3. Update customer count
4. Save
5. Forecast automatically updates!

## 📈 Best Practices

- **Be realistic** - Base on your sales pipeline
- **Review monthly** - Adjust as plans change
- **Track actuals** - Compare to projections
- **Document assumptions** - Use notes field
- **Plan conservatively** - Better to exceed than miss
