# Product Overview

Berry Brazil Açai is a sales and inventory management system designed for açai product distribution. The application serves two primary user groups:

**Field Sales (Mobile-First)**
- Real-time sales dashboard and analytics
- Quick point-of-sale interface for transactions
- Order tracking and management
- Stock level monitoring on the go

**Warehouse Staff (Desktop-Optimized)**
- Incoming stock receiving and management
- Stock adjustments and physical counts
- Stock movement history tracking
- Product catalog management

**ERP Features**
- Sales forecasting by product category
- Trend analysis and projections
- Smart reorder recommendations based on historical data
- Category performance analytics

## Business Context

- Currency: Australian Dollars (AUD)
- Product categories: Bowls, Smoothies, Powder, Frozen Packs
- Deployment: 100% serverless on AWS (no local servers)
- Access: Public URL accessible from any device with a browser
